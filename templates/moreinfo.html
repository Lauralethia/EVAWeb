3. Describe your tasks.

Classic laboratory-based examples:

  • Inhibiting a button press to a trained stimulus

Partially naturalistic examples:

  • Selective attention task conducted in virtual reality or a room resembling a naturalistic context (e.g. kitchen, a flat or a simple shop)

  • Classroom based and teacher lead cirriculum
